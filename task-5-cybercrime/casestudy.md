# Case Study - WannaCry Ransomware Attack

The most famous ransomware attack in the history of cyber attacks is the WannaCry ransomware attack, which began on the 12th of May, 2017. This ransomware spread very fast all over the world and affected computers in more than 150 countries.

The ransomware targeted Windows operating systems and exploited a known vulnerability in the Windows operating system. Once the ransomware attacked the computer, it encrypted all the data and displayed a ransom note that asked for a payment of $300 in Bitcoin to decrypt the data.

The WannaCry ransomware spread very fast and affected more than 200,000 computers all over the world. The most affected by the WannaCry ransomware were the hospitals in the United Kingdom, where the National Health Service (NHS) was forced to cancel thousands of appointments because of the ransomware attacks.

The WannaCry ransomware mostly affected those Windows operating systems that were not updated with the latest security patches. Although the fix for the WannaCry ransomware was available, many users were not aware of it and were affected by the ransomware.

This is the power of ransomware, which affected many organizations all over the world and forced them to pay the ransom to the attackers.
