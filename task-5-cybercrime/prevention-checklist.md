# Stay Safe Online – Ransomware Prevention Checklist
- Do not download files or software from unknown websites
- Do not open suspicious email attachments or links
- Always keep your operating system and software up to date
- Install good antivirus software and keep it updated
- Regularly back up your important data on cloud storage or external devices
- Do not click on pop-ups asking you to install unknown software
- Always use strong and unique passwords for your accounts
- Enable 2FA for your accounts
- Never send money or cryptocurrency to unknown people claiming to unlock your data
- Always check before making a transaction
- Immediately disconnect your device from the internet if you suspect a ransomware attack
- Report cyber crime at https://www.cybercrime.gov.in or call 1930
