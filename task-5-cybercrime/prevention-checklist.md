# Stay Safe Online – Ransomware Prevention Checklist
- Do not download files or software from untrusted or unknown websites
- Avoid opening suspicious email attachments or links
- Always keep your operating system and software updated
- Install reliable antivirus software and keep it updated
- Regularly back up your important files on cloud or external storage
- Do not click on pop-ups that ask you to install unknown software
- Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible
- Never send money or cryptocurrency to unknown sources claiming to unlock files
- Always verify any payment request before making a transaction
- Immediately disconnect your device from the internet if you suspect an attack
- Report cybercrime at https://www.cybercrime.gov.in or call 1930
