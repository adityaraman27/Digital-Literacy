I created my Digital Literacy infographic using Canva, as it provides an easy-to-use interface along with a variety of templates, icons, and design elements suitable for 
creating visually appealing content. The infographic is titled “Digital Literacy: A Smart Student Guide” and focuses on three key areas: understanding digital literacy,
useful digital tools for students, and maintaining a professional online presence.

In the first section, I explained digital literacy as the ability to effectively use digital tools for communication, learning, and information sharing, while also emphasizing safety and responsible usage. The second section highlights important tools such as Google Docs, Canva, GitHub, and online meeting platforms, which help students
in academic and collaborative work. The third section focuses on building a professional online presence by maintaining clean profiles, using platforms like LinkedIn and GitHub, and sharing achievements responsibly.
