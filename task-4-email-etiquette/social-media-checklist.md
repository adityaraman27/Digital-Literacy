# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist
Do’s
-Maintain a professional and respectful tone
-Share useful and meaningful content
-Verify information before posting
-Use privacy settings to control visibility
-Engage positively with others

Don’ts
-Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content
-Do not share personal or sensitive information
-Avoid spreading fake news or rumors
-Do not engage in online arguments or trolling
-Avoid excessive use of social media
