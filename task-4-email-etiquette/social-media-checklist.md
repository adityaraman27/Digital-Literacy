# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist
Do’s
- Be professional and respectful
- Share useful and meaningful content
- Ensure information is verified before posting
- Use privacy settings for better control
- Be positive when interacting with others

Don’ts
- Avoid posting offensive and inappropriate content
- Do not share personal information
- Avoid posting any form of fake news and rumors
- Do not engage in online arguments and trolling
- Avoid using social media too much
